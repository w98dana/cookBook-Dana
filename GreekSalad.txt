Ingredients:200 g cherry tomatoes, halved1 cucumber, diced1 red onion, thinly sliced1 green bell pepper, diced100 g feta cheese, crumbled2 tbsp olive oil1 tbsp red wine vinegar1 tsp dried oreganoSalt and pepper to tastePreparation:Combine tomatoes, cucumber, onion, bell pepper, feta, and olives in a bowl.Whisk together olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.Pour dressing over salad and toss to combine.Serve immediately.
